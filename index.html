Frequently Asked Questions

Our courses are designed for various skill levels. For beginner courses like "Intro to Pentesting," no prior experience is required. Advanced courses may require foundational knowledge in networking, programming, or prior pentesting experience.

Are the courses online or in-person?

All of our courses are offered online, providing flexibility to learn at your own pace from anywhere in the world. Some courses may also have live virtual sessions for more interactive learning.

Do the courses include hands-on labs?

Yes, all courses include hands-on labs and practical exercises to ensure you gain real-world experience with the tools and techniques covered.

How do I access the course materials?

Once you enroll, you will receive access to our online learning platform, where you can find all the course materials, including videos, reading materials, and lab exercises.
